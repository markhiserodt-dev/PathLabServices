About PathLab Services

PathLab Services is a growing pathology lab located in Southern California. It started out with a simple plan - to become a pathology lab that offered a variety of tests for doctors to order from. Since it's incubation, it has become much more. It is now the home to more than 50 employees, all dedicated to delivering the best results. PathLab also offers over 100 tests to get you the right results you need for you patient. We know time is important so we strive to get these test results back to you as soon as possible. Not only are we quick, but we have the highest quality laboratory machinery, so rest assured you can have confidence that the results are accurate.

With the whole world being affected by Covid, we wanted to do our part in any way we can to help relieve the stress it has caused. That is why we started offering Covid tests. We were one of the first labs in Southern California to offer Covid-19 testing, and have been a leader in delivering these tests. Our Covid tests are 99.9% accurate and results delivered within a few days.
